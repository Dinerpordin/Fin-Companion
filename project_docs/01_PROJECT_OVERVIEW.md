# Project Overview

## Vision

Build the most trusted free financial comparison and guidance platform in Bangladesh for non-privileged and underserved users.

## Mission

Help ordinary Bangladeshis privately understand loans, savings, and spending; compare better options; and navigate next steps using plain Bangla, transparent data, and non-advisory guidance.

## Core principles

- Bangla-first, English toggle optional.
- Privacy by default.
- No login required in Phase 1.
- Information only; no personalised financial advice.
- Transparent data sourcing with last-verified dates.
- Impartial comparison and clearly labelled sponsorship in later phases.
- Mobile-first with support for low-bandwidth and basic-phone users.

## Primary modules

1. Loan Reality Checker
2. Product Comparison Hub
3. Financial Health Assessment
4. Free Accounting Tool
5. What-If Scenario Planner
6. Locator for nearest bank, MFI, agent, or branch
7. Document Pack Generator
8. AI Financial Companion (text + voice)

## Target users

### Segment A: Rural and semi-urban borrowers

Users with informal or expensive borrowing who need help understanding true repayment cost and identifying nearby formal alternatives.

### Segment B: Low-literacy smartphone users

Users who can use a phone but struggle with forms, English-first apps, or financial jargon.

### Segment C: Women, youth, and first-time savers

Users who need product discovery, simple explanations, and guided next steps.

### Segment D: Remittance-linked households

Users who need help comparing savings, FD, and DPS products after receiving money.

### Segment E: Salaried or urban borrowers

Users comparing personal loans, credit cards, or refinance options and needing document guidance.

## Product promise

The platform tells users what a product costs, what alternatives exist, where to get them, and what documents are usually needed. It does not tell users what they should choose.

## Differentiators

- Bangla-first experience.
- Client-side privacy-first calculations.
- Real loan cost visibility.
- Full DPS and savings comparison focus.
- Free local-first cashbook.
- Guided but non-advisory AI assistant.
- Open, transparent source attribution.

## Phase 1 scope

Phase 1 focuses on trusted public information, calculators, comparison, education, and guided access. It does not process transactions, hold customer funds, or provide regulated financial advice.

## Phase 2 direction

After trust, usage, and compliance maturity are established, the platform can add partnerships, application flows, sponsored placements with clear labels, white-label APIs, and statistical data products.
