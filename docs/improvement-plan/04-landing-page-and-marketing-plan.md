# 04 - Landing Page & Marketing Plan

This plan establishes a marketing framework to build user trust, increase engagement, and drive adoption for the platform.

---

## Audience & Positioning

### Target Audience
* **Micro-entrepreneurs & Shopkeepers**: Need to track cash flow (cashbook) and avoid high-interest loans.
* **Rural & Semi-urban Savers**: Seek simple, Shariah-compliant comparison tables for DPS or FDR accounts.
* **Low-literacy & First-time Financial Consumers**: Rely on voice search and simple calculations.

### Jobs-to-be-Done (JTBD)
1. *“I need to understand the true cost of a loan before signing, without being forced to log in or share my personal details.”*
2. *“I need to record my daily sales and expenses locally on my phone without carrying a paper ledger.”*
3. *“I need to get clear financial answers in Bangla by speaking directly into my phone.”*

### Messaging Hierarchy
* **Primary Header**: "আপনার আর্থিক সিদ্ধান্ত হোক সহজ ও নিরাপদ" (Make Your Financial Decisions Simple and Safe).
* **Core Promise**: "ঋণ যাচাই থেকে সঞ্চয় তুলনা — কোনো লগইন বা ফি ছাড়াই, সম্পূর্ণ বাংলায়।" (From loan checking to savings comparison — without login or fees, entirely in Bangla.)
* **Trust Pillars**:
  1. *সম্পূর্ণ গোপনীয়* (Completely Confidential): Inputs remain on the user's device.
  2. *নিরপেক্ষ ও নির্ভুল* (Impartial & Accurate): No sales pitch, purely deterministic rates and verified data sources.
  3. *ভয়েস সহায়ক* (Voice-Enabled Assistant): Speak to ask questions and listen to answers.

## Call-to-Action (CTA) Strategy
* **Primary CTA**: "এখনই ব্যবহার শুরু করুন" (Start using now) — redirects directly to `/check-loan` or `/companion`.
* **Secondary CTA**: "কীভাবে কাজ করে দেখুন" (Watch how it works) — triggers a modal with an audio-guided tour of the calculators and ledger.

---

## Trust & Proof Requirements
* **Official Data Seals**: Highlight that product rates are pulled directly from Bangladesh Bank or commercial bank portals.
* **Privacy Assurance**: Display security icons next to the cashbook and calculator inputs to remind users that no data leaves their device.
* **Impartiality Notice**: Display a clear notice stating: *"আমরা কোনো ব্যাংক বা এনজিও নই, আমরা কোনো আর্থিক পণ্য বিক্রি করি না।"* (We are not a bank or NGO; we do not sell financial products.)

---

## Required Marketing & Showcase Assets
1. **App Viewport Screenshots**: High-contrast, clean screenshots of the step-by-step loan check wizard and the binned financial health status flags.
2. **Visual Flow Diagrams**: Simple diagrams showing how voice search works (User speaks -> Voice transcribed -> Impartial answer displayed -> Text read aloud).
3. **User Demonstration Storyboards**:
   * *Scene 1*: A rural farmer inputs loan details to spot high hidden fees.
   * *Scene 2*: A small shopkeeper logs a transaction in the cashbook with two taps.
   * *Scene 3*: A user asks the AI companion about agricultural loan eligibility using voice commands.
